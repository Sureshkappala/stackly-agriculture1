TIMELINE

The Evolution of Agri-Cloud

2020

Founding & Seed Funding

Stackly launches its first pilot project in California, coordinating real-time water metrics for 2,000 hectares of vineyards.

2022

Global Network Expansion

We deploy regional data nodes in Europe (Netherlands) and Oceania (Australia) to serve international precision growers.

2024

Green Grid Migration

Transitioned 100% of server computations to solar-powered microgrids co-located next to automated agricultural fields.

2026

Agri-Cloud Edge Integration

Unveiled next-gen low-latency telemetry databases supporting sub-millisecond offline syncing for autonomous machinery.

SUSTAINABILITY

Zero Net-Carbon Farming Cloud

Unlike legacy cloud systems that place massive thermal loads on regional power systems, Stackly data nodes are designed to exist in symbiosis with local farmlands.

By co-locating servers next to crop nurseries and utilizing automated greenhouse cooling vents, we utilize waste heat to nourish local crop yields.
